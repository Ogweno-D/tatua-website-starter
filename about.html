Our Story

Founded in 2020 by former enterprise support leaders, Tatua was born from frustration with outdated ticketing systems. We've processed over 2 million support tickets and helped companies reduce response times by an average of 45%.

After experiencing the pain of managing support operations at scale, our founders knew there had to a better way. Traditional ticketing systems were slow, inflexible, and created more problems than they solved.

Our Mission

To eliminate support chaos and help businesses deliver exceptional customer experience through intelligent automation and streamlined workflows.
